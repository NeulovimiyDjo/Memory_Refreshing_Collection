<p><b>Eldritch Blast</b> is an <a href="/wiki/Evocation" title="Evocation" class="mw-redirect">evocation</a> <a href="/wiki/Cantrip" title="Cantrip" class="mw-redirect">cantrip</a> available to <a href="/wiki/Warlock" title="Warlock">warlocks</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-spellLists_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-spellLists-1">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Description"> Description </span></h2>
<p>You use your action to create a beam of magical energy and hurl it at a <a href="/wiki/Creature" title="Creature">creature</a> you can see within range. Make a ranged spell attack. On hit, the target takes 1d10 <a href="/wiki/Force" title="Force" class="mw-redirect">force</a> damage.<sup id="cite_ref-spellDesc_0-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-spellDesc-0">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Leveling">Leveling</span></h3>
<p>The spell creates more beams as you level. At 5th, it makes two beams; at 11th, it makes three; and at 17th, it makes four. The beams can be directed at the same target or different ones. Each beam gets its own attack roll.<sup id="cite_ref-spellDesc_0-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-spellDesc-0">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
